Carmen 'Carmy' Berzatto - Head Chef

Carmen Anthony "Carmy" Berzatto is the main character in the first and second seasons of the comedy drama series The Bear. He is portrayed by Jeremy Allen White.
A Chicago native and award-winning chef, Carmy leaves the world of fine dining to take control of his family's restaurant, following the suicide of his older brother Mikey. While his efforts to revitalize the restaurant are largely successful, he ultimately closes down the restaurant in favor of opening a new one.

Sydney Adamu - Sous Chef

Sydney "Syd" Adamu is a main character in the comedy drama series The Bear. She is a sous-chef at The Original Beef of Chicagoland, and helps Carmy Berzatto fix the restaurant. She is portrayed by Ayo Edebiri.
